Welcome to Terence's online APUSH notes.

I strive to create thorough, well prepared notes that will focus on building conceptual understanding while densely packing in specific examples to illustrate the ideas. I hope this will serve as a reliable and helpful resources for any student taking Advanced Placement United States History, as well as a catalyst for starting to appreciate and enjoy the complexity of history.

Period 1: 1491-1607

Not covered in the 2020 AP US History Exam

Period 2: 1607-1754

Not covered in the 2020 AP US History Exam

Period 3: 1754-1800

This period looks at how America gained its independence and its attempts at a Democratic and Republican government.

Period 4: 1800-1848

This period looks at how America reinforced democratic values, underwent a period of industrialization, established new cultures especially with a religious revivalism, and had increasingly sectional politics.

Period 5: 1844-1877

This period looks at how America expanded, got into a civil war, and looked to develop in its aftermath.

Period 6: 1865-1898

This period looks at how America embraced a capitalist market, integrated immigrants, developed new cultures, and created some political arguments over the highly industrialized society.

Period 7: 1890-1945

This period looks at how America came to dominate international politics, create reforms to raise social standards, fostered a new national culture, and the creation of modern American elements.
